We're a student-run VEX IQ team that takes full ownership of everything — robot design, programming, the engineering notebook, and our match strategy. No outside builds. No shortcuts. Everything you see came from us.

This season we're competing in VEX IQ Level Up. Our goal is simple: show up to every event with a robot that's better than it was the week before, and a team that knows exactly how to use it.

OUR GOALS

01
Win the Halloween Qualifier
November 1st is our first real test. We want to go in with a polished robot, a full autonomous routine, and no surprises.
02
Complete the Engineering Notebook
Our notebook needs to tell the full story — every design decision, every test, and why we made the calls we did. Judges read every page.
03
Film a Reveal Video
We want to put out a proper reveal video before the qualifier — something that shows the robot in action and makes people take us seriously.
04
Score Consistently in Autonomous
Autonomous is where matches are decided at high levels. We're building a routine that runs clean every single time, not just in practice.
05
Launch Team Merch
We're working on team gear — jerseys, hoodies, or hats. Something that looks good at events and helps sponsors see us as a real organization.
06
Keep This Website Updated
Every build log, every result, every media post goes here. We want judges, parents, and sponsors to find real work — not a blank page.
