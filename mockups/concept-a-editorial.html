Who We Are

Students who build,
break, and
build again.

Team 839A is a student-led VEX IQ robotics team competing at regional, state, and world championship levels. Every member owns a piece of the robot — from CAD and code to drive strategy and outreach.

We believe the best engineering happens when curiosity meets persistence. Every failed prototype is a step toward a better design.
