Team 839A is a student-led VEX IQ competitive robotics team. Every member owns a role — from mechanical design and programming to match strategy and community outreach. We compete at regional, state, and world championship levels.

We don't just build robots. We build the engineers who build them. Every season, we return with a better team, a better machine, and a sharper edge.
