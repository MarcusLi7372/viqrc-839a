About the Team

Team 839A is a student-led VEX IQ team competing at regional, state, and world championship levels. Every member owns a clear role — from mechanical design and programming to match strategy and community outreach.

We believe engineering is a mindset, not just a skill. Every robot we build carries the lessons of the one before it.
