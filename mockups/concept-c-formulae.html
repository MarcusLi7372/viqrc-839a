About the Team

Team 839A is a student-led VEX IQ competitive robotics team. Every member owns a clear role — from mechanical design and programming to match strategy and community outreach. We compete at regional, state, and world championship levels.

Each season, we carry the lessons of every robot we've built before. Every iteration makes us faster, more precise, and more competitive.
