Engineering Decisions

DESIGN NOTES

01
Mecanum Drive
We chose mecanum wheels because Level Up requires precise lateral positioning when scoring. Strafing in and out of scoring zones is faster than tank-style pivoting under match pressure.
02
Flex Wheel Intake
Flex wheels grip game pieces without crushing them. v1 used hard rollers that dropped pieces on contact. v2 runs cleaner at high speed and handles pieces at any angle of approach.
03
4-Bar Lift
A 4-bar keeps the scoring mechanism level throughout the lift range. This lets us score without re-aligning the piece at height, which saves time per cycle and reduces drop rate.
04
Autonomous Path
We're building a full-field autonomous routine using sensor-based correction. Pure driver practice won't be enough at Provincials level — autonomous points matter every match.
